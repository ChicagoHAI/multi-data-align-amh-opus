# Downloaded Papers

This directory contains 7 research papers relevant to multimodal data alignment techniques for adolescent mental health AI modeling.

## Papers List

### 1. Multimodal Machine Learning in Mental Health: A Survey (2024)
- **File**: [2407.16804_multimodal_ml_mental_health_survey.pdf](2407.16804_multimodal_ml_mental_health_survey.pdf)
- **Authors**: Zahraa Al Sahili, Ioannis Patras, Matthew Purver
- **Year**: 2024 (revised June 2025)
- **arXiv**: 2407.16804
- **Size**: 471K
- **Why relevant**: Comprehensive survey cataloging 26 public datasets and comparing 28 multimodal models for mental health. Systematically compares transformer, graph, and hybrid-based fusion strategies. Directly addresses data alignment challenges.

### 2. Digital Phenotyping for Adolescent Mental Health (2025)
- **File**: [2501.08851_digital_phenotyping_adolescent.pdf](2501.08851_digital_phenotyping_adolescent.pdf)
- **Authors**: Balasundaram Kadirvelu, Teresa Bellido Bel, Aglaia Freccero, Martina Di Simplicio, Dasha Nicholls, A Aldo Faisal
- **Year**: 2025

- **Size**: 1.7M

## Summary

These papers cover:
- **Multimodal fusion techniques**: Cross-attention, transformer-based, graph-based, hybrid approaches
- **Mental health applications**: Depression, anxiety, eating disorders, suicidal ideation, insomnia
- **Target population**: Adolescent and youth mental health
- **Data modalities**: Text, audio/voice, video/facial expressions, smartphone sensors, physiological signals, neuroimaging
- **Alignment methods**: Cross-modal alignment, variational copula, adaptive experts, attention mechanisms
- **Datasets**: Comprehensive reviews of available mental health datasets
